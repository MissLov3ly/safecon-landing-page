FAQ

What is Safe{Con}?

Organised by Safe (prev. Gnosis Safe), Safe{Con} is an annual web3 conference for builders held in Berlin, Germany. The one-day event is a unique blend of announcements, workshops and engagements focused on accelerating the transition from externally owned user accounts to smart accounts, also known as smart contract-based wallets. The event aims to foster a community to take ownership of the transition from EOAs to smart accounts and contribute to the broader Ethereum ecosystem.

Is Safe{Con} free?

Yes, attendance is free of charge, but participants must first register, with their registration subject to approval by the organiser.
